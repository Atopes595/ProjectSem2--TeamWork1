Fruit Salad Recipe
Ingredients (Serves 4)
1 fresh mango (peeled, pitted, cubed)
1 cup strawberries (hulled, sliced)
1 medium banana (sliced, fresh)
1 cup blueberries (rinsed)
1 cup green grapes (halved, optional)
½ cup pineapple chunks (fresh or canned, drained)
2 tbsp honey (or maple syrup for vegan)
1 tsp fresh lemon juice
A pinch of cinnamon (optional, for extra flavor)
Instructions
Rinse all fresh fruits thoroughly under cold water; pat dry strawberries and blueberries with a paper towel.
Peel and cut mango, pineapple into bite-sized cubes; hull and slice strawberries; halve grapes if using. Slice banana right before mixing to avoid browning.
In a large mixing bowl, combine all prepared fruits.
In a small bowl, stir together honey, fresh lemon juice and a pinch of cinnamon (if using) until well mixed.
Drizzle the honey-lemon dressing over the fruit salad and gently toss to coat all fruits evenly.
Chill the fruit salad in the refrigerator for 10-15 minutes (optional) to enhance the flavor.
Serve cold and enjoy!
Tips
Add other fruits like kiwi, orange segments or raspberries based on your taste.
For a creamy version, mix in 2 tbsp of plain yogurt or coconut yogurt before serving.
Do not slice the banana too early—add it just before tossing to keep it fresh and yellow.
